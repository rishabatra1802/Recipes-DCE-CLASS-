Cheesecake

Cheesecake is a rich and creamy dessert with a smooth texture and a deliciously sweet flavor. It is often made with a base of cream cheese, eggs, and sugar, set atop a buttery graham cracker crust.

This classic dessert is perfect for special occasions or as a delightful treat any time of year. You can top it with fresh fruit, chocolate, or your favorite sauce for extra indulgence.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 2 cups cream cheese (softened)
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup sour cream

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 325°F (163°C). Grease a 9-inch springform pan.
  2. Mix graham cracker crumbs and melted butter. Press into the bottom of the pan to form the crust.
  3. In a bowl, beat cream cheese and sugar until smooth. Add eggs one at a time, mixing well after each.
  4. Stir in vanilla extract and sour cream until combined.
  5. Pour the filling over the crust and smooth the top.
  6. Bake for 50-60 minutes, or until the center is almost set. Let cool, then refrigerate for at least 4 hours before serving.
